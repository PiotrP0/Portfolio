About Us

We are a small family business that started in 1980 in Poland. As a young boy Artur was making bracelets from limestone crystals that could be found nearby limestone quarry, near his home. Later in life he was improving his art by making pendants, earrings and bracelets from various materials including semi precious stones. Years later after moving to the UK, he continued his art with knowledge of how to produce the best jewelry with the most lasting materials. Today you can meet him on Surrey Street Market in Croydon.
